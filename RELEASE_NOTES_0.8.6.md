## üîÑ Upgrading from v0.8.5

**Fully backwards compatible!** Your custom messages and settings are preserved.

### Getting the New 30 Messages

If you're upgrading from v0.8.5, you have three options:

1. **Use the new Restore Defaults button** (Recommended)
   - Right-click [LT] icon ‚Üí Open editor
   - Click "Restore" button
   - Confirm to replace your messages with the new 30 defaults

2. **Delete your messages.txt and restart**
   - The module will auto-create the new defaults

3. **Keep your existing messages**
   - Your current messages continue to work perfectly
   - No action needed

## üì• Installation

### New Users
1. Download `bh.lt.messages_0.8.6.bhm` from this release
2. Place it in: `Documents\Guild Wars 2\addons\blishhud\modules\`
3. Restart Blish HUD
4. Enable the module
5. Look for the [LT] icon in Blish HUD corner menu

### Upgrading from v0.8.5
1. Exit Blish HUD
2. Replace your old .bhm file with `bh.lt.messages_0.8.6.bhm`
3. Restart Blish HUD
4. Use "Restore" button in editor to get the new 30 messages (optional)
